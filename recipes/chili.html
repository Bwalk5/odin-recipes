How to make legendary chili

Description

Chili is a gift from the universe. It should be serve often with cheese and/or sour cream

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ¾ cup diced onion
  • ¾ cup diced celery
  • ¾ cup diced green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans tomato puree
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans with liquid
  • ½ tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¾ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Steps for Success

  1. Place beef in a skillet over medium heat and cook until evenly brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain grease.

  2. Transfer beef to a slow cooker and mix in onion, celery, bell pepper, garlic, tomato puree, kidney beans, and cannellini beans. Season with chili powder, salt, basil, oregano, parsley, black pepper, and hot pepper sauce.Directions Instructions Checklist

  3. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours.
